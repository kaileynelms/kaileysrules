Kailey's Rules

(By Kailey)

  

1) Stop ignoring your artistic talents in order to fit in

2) Accept that you see the world through a more creative lens

3) Use your weaknesses to create meaningful art

4) To be able to create, is to be free

5) Be proud of everything you make, whether you think it has meaning or not

6) Get ready to work hard and be patient

7) Don’t wait until you know who you are to get started

8) Art is whatever you say it is

9) Don’t be afraid of criticism

10) You’re capable, you just have to believe it
